The Challenge: From Reactive Repairs to Proactive Uptime

Unplanned downtime is one of the biggest hidden costs in industrial operations. A single component failure can bring an entire production line or oil rig to a halt. Traditional maintenance schedules are often based on simple timelines, not the actual condition of the equipment, leading to either premature replacement of healthy parts or catastrophic failures that could have been predicted.

The Solution: Deploying a "Predictive Uptime" Model

ahatis builds a custom AI model that ingests data from equipment sensors, maintenance logs, and environmental factors to move beyond simple failure prediction. The model identifies not just individual parts at risk, but entire component clusters that are statistically likely to fail in the same window. This allows your maintenance teams to bundle repairs, replacing all at-risk components during a single, planned downtime event.

Recognizing that many critical assets operate in remote locations, ahatis has partnered with a leading AI firm, OpenInfer, to build this solution to function without an internet connection, ensuring these predictive capabilities are always available.

We manage the entire custom build and, upon completion, provide comprehensive training and documentation for a smooth transition to your internal team, allowing you to own and operate the model to reduce its total cost of ownership (TCO). Alternatively, ahatis can provide ongoing maintenance.

Targeted Outcomes: The Value Proposition

  • 20-30% Target Reduction in Unplanned Downtime: Proactively address potential failures before they bring operations to a halt.
  • 10-15% Target Reduction in Maintenance Costs: Optimize maintenance schedules and reduce emergency repair expenditures.
  • Increased Asset Lifespan: Make smarter decisions about component replacement to get the most out of your equipment.
  • Optimized Maintenance Scheduling: Group repairs intelligently to minimize the frequency and duration of scheduled downtime.

The Industry Challenge: Disconnected Settlement Amounts on Claims

Across the insurance industry, a critical challenge persists. The original settlement amount often under-represents what the customer is authorized. A common finding is that average claim cycle times can extend to 28 days or more, with a significant portion of that time—often over 60%—spent on administrative back-and-forth to correct incomplete or inaccurate initial submissions. Because of these inaccuracies the customer (aka home owner) often is impatient of uniformed of the appraisal process which can get them the settlement they rightfully deserve.

The Solution: A Proactive Partnership for "Intelligent Intake"

Recognizing this market need, the joint team of ahatis and MCA (My Claims Appraisal) are co-creating a bespoke AI-driven platform called "Intelligent Intake." This solution, which combines leading edge AI and data architecture expertise with MCA's deep industry knowledge, is being built to integrate seamlessly into the existing appraisal process. The system is designed to act as an intelligent co-pilot for policyholders and contractors, validating information in real-time to ensure every appraisal is 100% complete and accurate before it ever reaches the final award.

This proactive development will provide a market-ready solution that can be customized and deployed for home owners and contractors, enabling them to rapidly modernize their claims process.

Targeted Outcomes: The Value Proposition

  • 50% Target Reduction in Cycle Time: The platform's goal is to reduce the average appraisal settlement time from 60 days to just 30 days.
  • 35% Target Decrease in Administrative Costs: The automation of the appraisal process is projected to significantly reduce the human hours required per appraisal.
  • 40% Target Improvement in Settlement Amount: The technology enabled appraisal process is designed to provide the fairest settlement in the industry.
  • Straight-Through Processing: The platform aims to enable over 80% of property appraisal steps to be processed automatically, without the need for extensive human interaction.
